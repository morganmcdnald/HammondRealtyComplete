Welcome to Hammond Realty

Your Dream. Our Commitment.

Real estate is a powerful thing. Where you live becomes the center of your universe; your “home base” and a place where memories are made. Even an investment property can have a profound effect on your life’s story—when chosen correctly, it becomes long-term financial freedom and a vital part of your well-diversified portfolio.

Anchored by Joel Hammond, Sheila Hammond, and Matt Quinn, allow Hammond Realty to guide you through the real estate process. Whether a buyer or seller, we think of our clients as family. Lean on us—we are here to keep the process fluid, successful, and as low-stress as possible.

Thanks to a combined 60 years as real estate professionals in the Santa Clarita Valley, we are proud of our reputation for excellent negotiation skills, cutting-edge marketing, and invaluable connections, both locally and globally.
